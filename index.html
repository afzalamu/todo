Weekly Fat-Loss & Strength Routine

Custom balanced schedule for PG + 9–5 lifestyle

Monday — Upper Body Strength

  • Bench Press — 4x10
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press — 3x12
  • Lat Pulldown — 3x10
  • Biceps Curls + Triceps Pushdown superset — 3x15 each

Diet: High protein + moderate carbs. Avoid fried food.

Tuesday — Lower Body Focus

  • Squats — 4x10
  • Leg Press — 3x12
  • Walking Lunges — 3 sets
  • Calf Raises — 4x20

Cardio: 10-min incline walk post-workout

Wednesday — Active Recovery & Core

  • Plank — 3x60s
  • Hanging Leg Raises — 3x15
  • Stretching + Foam Rolling — 15 min
  • Optional light jog or walk — 20 min

Thursday — Push Day

  • Incline Bench Press — 4x8
  • Overhead Shoulder Press — 3x10
  • Triceps Dips — 3x12

Diet: Keep carbs moderate and increase protein (chicken, paneer, tofu).

Friday — Pull Day

  • Pull-Ups — 3x8
  • Seated Row — 4x10
  • Face Pulls — 3x12
  • Hammer Curls — 3x12

Cardio: 15-min cycling at steady pace

Saturday — Full Body Circuit + HIIT

  • Burpees — 3x12
  • Mountain Climbers — 3x40s
  • Push-Ups + Squat combo — 3 rounds
  • Skipping — 5x1min intervals

Diet: Lighter meals. Add green tea and fruits.

Sunday — Rest & Planning

  • Stretch and Mobility work — 10 min
  • Meal prep for week
  • Reflect on previous week’s progress

Note: Hydrate well and aim for 7–8 hrs sleep.
