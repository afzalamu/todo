Weekly Fat-Loss & Strength Plan

PG Routine + 9–5 Compatible | Scrolls Automatically to Today

Monday — Upper Body Strength

Workout:
  • Push-ups – 3×15
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3×12
  • Pull-ups / Lat Pull-down – 3×10
  • Plank – 3×45s hold
Diet Focus:
  • Breakfast: Eggs, oats, green tea
  • Lunch: Brown rice + chicken + salad
  • Dinner: Grilled paneer/tofu + soup

Tuesday — Cardio + Core

Workout:
  • Jogging / Jump rope – 20 mins
  • Russian twists – 3×20
  • Leg raises – 3×15
  • Mountain climbers – 3×30s
Diet Focus:
  • Focus on hydration and fruits.
  • Keep carbs moderate, protein-rich meals.

Wednesday — Core + Stability

Workout:
  • Side planks – 3×30s each
  • Plank reaches – 3×15
  • Crunches – 3×25
Diet Focus:
  • Include clean carbs (oats, brown rice).
  • Protein shakes or sprouts mid-day.

Thursday — Lower Body Strength

Workout:
  • Squats – 4×12
  • Lunges – 3×10 each leg
  • Calf raises – 3×15
  • Glute bridge – 3×15
Diet Focus:
  • High-protein intake today (chicken, paneer, lentils).
  • Hydrate well and stretch post-workout.

Friday — Full Body Circuit

Workout:
  • Burpees – 3×12
  • Jump squats – 3×15
  • Push-ups – 3×15
  • Plank hold – 3×45s
Diet Focus:
  • Balanced day: protein, carbs, fats in proportion.
  • Early dinner and 7+ hrs of sleep.

Saturday — Mobility + Outdoor Activity

Workout:
  • Yoga / Mobility drills – 30 mins
  • Outdoor walk / cycling
Diet Focus:
  • Fresh fruits and veggies.
  • Light meals — focus on recovery.

Sunday — Rest + Recharge

Activities:
  • Stretch and relax.
  • Plan upcoming week.
  • Hydrate and stay off screens.
Diet Focus:
  • Clean food but one treat meal allowed.

Quick Tips

  • Stay consistent 80% of the week.
  • Drink 2–3L water daily.
  • Sleep 7–8 hours regularly.
  • Track your meals & progress weekly.
